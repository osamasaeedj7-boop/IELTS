IELTS Speaking

Lesson 7

Natural Speaking &
Band 6–7 Expressions

Lesson Aims

Today we level up. We will focus on how to:

1
Sound more natural (not robotic)
2
Avoid basic/simple answers
3
Add strong personalization
4
Use flexible speaking phrases

Quick Review

Good Answer =

  • Idea
  • Reason
  • Example
  • Natural language

Bad Answer =

  • Short
  • Repetitive
  • Basic
  • Unnatural

Today’s Focus

We are NOT learning new structures today.

We are learning how to sound natural and fluent.

1. Avoid Basic Answers

Weak Answer

"I like my job because it is good."

Better Answer

"I like my job because it gives me a sense of satisfaction, especially when I see the final results of my work."

Weak Answer

"I like English because it is important."

Better Answer

"I like English because it opens many opportunities for my future, especially for studying abroad."

Practice: Upgrade These Answers

I like my hobby because it is fun.

English is important.

I like my job.

To improve them, you must add:

Reason Detail Feeling

2. Use Natural Fillers

Fillers help you think, sound natural, and avoid silence.

Useful fillers (Fluency Boost):

Well, actually… To be honest… Let me think… I would say… It depends, but…

Model Answer

Q: Do you like studying alone?

"Well, actually, I prefer studying alone most of the time because I can focus better. However, sometimes I study with others when I want to share ideas."

3. Add Personalization Very Important

Band 6+ students always connect answers to their own life.

Weak

Social media is useful.

Better

In my case, social media is very useful because I use it for fashion content and communication.

Weak

English is important.

Better

For me, English is important because I want to study in Italy and continue my education there.

The Rule

"For me..." "In my case..." "Personally..."

4. Avoid Repetition & Expand

Repetitive Answer

"I like my job. I like this job because I like design. I like architecture."

Improved Answer

"I enjoy my job because it allows me to be creative, especially in design and architecture projects."

Instead of repeating words:

Use: IT / THIS / THAT

Instead of 1 sentence:

Idea Reason Example Result

Part 2 Upgrade

Natural Storytelling

Topic: Describe a person who helped you

Opening I would like to talk about my sister, who helped me a lot in choosing my career.

Context When I was in high school, I used to visit her at university, and I really liked the atmosphere there.

Detail She was studying architecture, and I was impressed by her work and projects.

Action Because of that, I decided to follow a similar path and study engineering.

Result Since then, I have been more focused on my goals, and I feel that it was a very important decision in my life.

Part 3 Upgrade

Band 6 Style Answer

Q: Why do some people succeed more than others?

Opinion I think some people succeed more than others because they are more disciplined and focused on their goals.

Example For example, people who study regularly and manage their time well are more likely to achieve better results.

Addition Another reason is that they stay motivated even when they face difficulties.

Result As a result, they can improve faster and reach their goals more easily.

Useful Vocabulary

discipline = disiplin

Ex: Self-discipline is important for success.

confidence = özgüven

Ex: Practice helps build confidence.

opportunity = fırsat

Ex: English gives many opportunities.

progress = ilerleme

Ex: Daily study leads to progress.

improve = geliştirmek

Ex: I want to improve my speaking.

focus = odaklanmak

Ex: I need to focus on one idea.

result = sonuç

Ex: Hard work gives good results.

habit = alışkanlık

Ex: Speaking daily is a good habit.

Useful Speaking Phrases

Well, actually… To be honest… I would say… In my case… For me… Because of that… As a result… Since then… One reason is that… Another reason is that…

Controlled Practice

Question 1

Why is English important for you?

Use:

  • for me
  • reason
  • example
Question 2

What helps people succeed?

Use:

  • two reasons
  • example
  • result
Question 3

Describe a person who influenced you.

Use:

story feeling result

Review + Homework

Today we learned:

  • how to sound natural
  • how to avoid basic answers
  • how to add personal ideas
  • how to expand answers
  • how to speak like Band 6

Homework

Recording 1:

Why is English important in your life?

Speak in 4–5 sentences.

Recording 2:

Describe a person who helped you in your life.

Speak for 1 minute.

Must use:

  • Well, actually…
  • In my case…
  • One reason is that…
  • For example…
  • As a result…
