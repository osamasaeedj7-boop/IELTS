Main Goals

Today we will focus on how to:

1
Speak longer in Part 3
2
Answer abstract questions more clearly
3
Build ideas step by step (reduce confusion)
4
Improve Opinion + Reason + Example + Result pattern

Warm-Up

Answer these 2 short personal questions:

1. Do you think learning English is easier now than before?

2. Is social media more helpful or more harmful?

Teacher Note

Today we are not focusing on basic sentence correction.

Today we are focusing on how to think and speak better in Part 3.

Main Teaching Point

Part 3 Answer Formula

1. Opinion 2. Reason 3. Example 4. Result

Opinion

  • • I think...
  • • In my opinion...
  • • I would say...

Reason

  • • One reason is that...
  • • This is because...
  • • The main reason is that...

Example

  • • For example...
  • • For instance...
  • • A good example is...

Result

  • • So...
  • • That’s why...
  • • As a result...

The Formula in Action

Q: Why do some people learn skills faster than others?

Opinion I think some people learn skills faster because they practise more regularly.

Reason One reason is that repeated practice helps people remember and improve.

Example For example, if someone speaks English every day, they usually become more fluent than a person who only studies once a week.

Result So overall, regular practice makes a big difference.

The Golden Rule

Do NOT answer with many disconnected ideas.

Give ONE strong idea first. Then develop it.

Bad Style (Too many ideas)

"Some people learn fast because skill is important and people work and job and teacher and family and social media and talent."

Better Style (One developed idea)

"I think some people learn faster because they practise more often. For example, when a person uses a skill every day, they improve more naturally. So practice is one of the biggest reasons."

How to Answer Abstract Questions

Part 1 = Personal Part 2 = Story Part 3 = Idea

In Part 3, you do not always need a personal story.

Model Answer

Q: Is influence always positive?

No, I don’t think influence is always positive. One reason is that people can also be affected by bad examples, especially online. For instance, some young people copy unhealthy habits from social media. So influence can be positive or negative depending on the person and situation.

Use General Examples

Use these safe, general subjects for your Part 3 examples instead of "I" or "my friend":

students young people families social media users workers teachers society daily life

Model Answer

Q: Do teachers influence students a lot?

Yes, I think teachers influence students a lot. One reason is that students spend a lot of time with teachers during important years of their life. For example, a good teacher can improve a student’s confidence and motivation. So teachers can have a strong effect on students’ future.

Mini Controlled Practice

We will build the answer together, step by step.

Question: Why do people use social media so much?

Teacher:
Start with opinion.
Student:
I think people use social media a lot nowadays.
Teacher:
Good. Give me one reason.
Student:
One reason is that it is easy and fast.
Teacher:
Good. Give me an example.
Student:
For example, people can watch videos and speak with friends.
Teacher:
Now finish.
Student:
So that is why social media is very common.

Short Structure Drill

Repeat this structure 3 times to build muscle memory:

1 I think...

2 One reason is that...

3 For example...

4 So overall...

Now, use this exact structure to answer 3 different questions.

High-Value Model Answers

Q: Is social media more helpful or more harmful?

In my opinion, social media is helpful in many ways, but it can also be harmful. One reason is that it helps people communicate quickly and learn new ideas. For example, many students use social media to find useful content and improve their knowledge. However, it can also waste time if people use it too much. So overall, I think it is useful if people use it carefully.

Q: Why do many people want to study abroad?

I think many people want to study abroad because it gives them better opportunities. One reason is that they can improve their education and also learn about a new culture. For example, a student who studies in another country may become more independent and confident. So overall, studying abroad can help people both personally and professionally.

Q: Should schools teach more practical skills?

Yes, I think schools should teach more practical skills. One reason is that students need useful abilities for real life, not only academic information. For example, communication, time management, and problem-solving are important in both work and daily life. So I believe practical skills should have a bigger place in education.

Review + Homework

Today's Most Important Rule:

Do NOT say many weak ideas.

Say ONE clear idea and develop it.

Best Speaking Formula:

Opinion + Reason + Example + Result

Voice Homework

Record these 3 questions:

  • 1. Why do some people become successful in their field?
  • 2. Do young people depend too much on technology?
  • 3. Should students learn communication skills at school?

Instructions:

  • Answer each in 4–6 sentences.
  • Start with opinion, give 1 reason, 1 example, finish with a result.
  • Do not jump between many ideas.

Keep It Clear.

In Part 3, your answer does not need to be complicated.

It needs to be clear.

  • First say your opinion.
  • Then give one reason.
  • Then give one example.
  • Then finish clearly.

This will make your speaking stronger and more confident!
