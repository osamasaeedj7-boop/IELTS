IELTS Speaking

Lesson 5

Building Stronger Part 3 Answers

Lesson Aims

Today we will focus on how to:

1
Give opinions more clearly
2
Compare past and present
3
Talk about advantages & disadvantages
4
Speak about the future with better structure

Quick Review

Part 2 Structure:

  • introduce the topic
  • give details
  • explain why it is important
  • explain the result or effect

Part 3 Structure:

  • opinion
  • reason
  • example
  • final sentence

Today’s Focus

We are not learning what Part 2 and Part 3 are.

We are learning how to answer them better.

Giving Opinions Clearly

Useful starters:

I think… In my opinion… I’d say… Personally, I think…

Follow-up language:

because… One reason is that… For example,… That’s why…

Model Answer

Q: Do you think technology helps students learn better?

I think technology helps students learn better because it gives them quick access to information. For example, students can watch videos, use learning apps, and find explanations online. That’s why technology can make learning easier and faster.

Practice: Giving Opinions

Answer these questions:

1

Do you think young people should learn practical skills?

2

Do you think social media is useful for learning?

Try to use:

I think… because… For example,… That’s why…

Comparing Past and Present

Useful language:

In the past… Nowadays… These days… Compared to the past…

Model Answer

Q: Do people learn differently now compared to the past?

Yes, I think they do. In the past, people mostly learned from books and teachers. Nowadays, they also learn from the internet, videos, and apps. Compared to the past, learning is now faster and more flexible.

Practice: Past and Present

Answer these questions:

1

Is life more stressful now than in the past?

2

Do students study differently now?

Try to use:

In the past… Nowadays… Compared to the past…

Advantages and Disadvantages

Useful language:

One advantage is that… Another advantage is that… One disadvantage is that… However,…

Model Answer

Q: What are the advantages of studying abroad?

One advantage is that students can improve their language skills. Another advantage is that they can become more independent. However, studying abroad can also be expensive and stressful.

Practice: Advantages & Disadvantages

Answer these questions:

1

What are the advantages of learning English?

2

What are the disadvantages of spending too much time on social media?

Try to use:

One advantage is that… Another advantage is that… One disadvantage is that… However,…

Talking About the Future

Useful language:

In the future… I think … will… It is likely that… People may…

Model Answer

Q: How do you think education will change in the future?

I think education will become more digital in the future. People may study more online and use more technology in class. It is likely that learning will become more flexible and personalized.

Practice: Talking About the Future

Answer these questions:

1

How will technology change people’s jobs in the future?

2

How will students learn English in the future?

Try to use:

In the future… I think … will… People may… It is likely that…

Part 3 Practice: Education & Learning

1

Why do some students learn faster than others?

2

What are the advantages of learning a foreign language?

3

How do you think education will change in the future?

Remember to use:

opinion reason example comparison / future / advantage language

Useful Vocabulary

education = eğitim

Ex: Education plays an important role in people’s lives.

practical = pratik, uygulamalı

Ex: Young people should learn practical skills for the future.

advantage = avantaj

Ex: One advantage of learning English is better job opportunities.

disadvantage = dezavantaj

Ex: One disadvantage of social media is wasting too much time.

flexible = esnek

Ex: Online learning is more flexible for many students.

independent = bağımsız

Ex: Studying abroad can make students more independent.

digital = dijital

Ex: Education is becoming more digital every year.

personalized = kişiselleştirilmiş

Ex: In the future, learning may become more personalized.

access = erişim

Ex: Technology gives students easy access to information.

opportunity = fırsat

Ex: Learning English gives people more opportunities.

Useful Speaking Phrases
