Lesson 8

Final Speaking Practice

Lesson Goal

Bring together the full course and answer more naturally from start to finish.

1
Open answers calmly
2
Develop ideas clearly
3
Use examples and reasons
4
Finish with confidence

Final Practice Tasks

Part 1

Talk about a daily habit that helps you stay productive.

Give a direct answer, one reason, and one short example.

Part 2

Describe a goal you are working toward now.

  • what the goal is
  • why it matters to you
  • what steps you are taking
  • why you think you can achieve it

Part 3

Why do some people stay motivated more easily than others?

Give your opinion, explain one reason, and include a simple example.

Review + Homework

Use this structure

  • answer clearly
  • add one reason
  • include one example
  • finish naturally

Homework

Record one full practice set using one Part 1 question, one Part 2 topic, and one Part 3 question.

Try to speak naturally, not perfectly.

Repeat once and improve one detail each time.
